(["'])(?:(?=(\\?))\2.)*?\1

(["'])		capture group of single or double quotes
(?:(?=(\\?))\2.)	Non-capturing group of (?=(\\?))\2.
(?=(\\?))		Positive lookahead of escape character (\\?)
\2			Backreference to \\?
.			Matches any character different from line breaks
*?			Non-greedy operator of (?:(?=(\\?))\2.)
\1			Backreference to ["']
